Chocolate Chip Cookies recipe

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 3/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 12 oz. bag of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 2 1/4 cups of flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

    • Directions

    Step 1 Step 2 Step 3 Step 4 Step 5
    Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Microwave butter for 2 minutes.
    Pour butter into a bowl with the sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
    Then mix in the flour, baking soda, salt, and chocolate chips.     		Scoop the dough and roll into balls.
    Spray the oven sheet with cooking spray then place the dough onto the sheet.    		 Cook for 12-16 minutes. ENJOY:)
